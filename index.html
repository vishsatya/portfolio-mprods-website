About Us

Manikyam Productions is a creative production company focused on character-driven stories and visually bold filmmaking.

Founded with a passion for storytelling, Manikyam Productions has grown from a small creative studio into a full-service film production company known for delivering compelling visual narratives. Our mission is to craft stories that inspire, entertain, and connect with audiences across cultures and platforms. Guided by a team of dedicated directors, producers, writers, and technical artists, we bring together creativity and precision at every stage of production. From independent films to large-scale commercial projects, our commitment remains the same—transforming ideas into cinematic experiences that leave a lasting impact.


Manikyam Productions: Where Stories Come Alive

At Manikyam Productions, we believe in the transformative power of storytelling. Through the lens of our cameras and the magic of cinematic artistry, we bring dreams to life, emotions to the forefront, and narratives that captivate the soul.

As a passionate team of filmmakers, we have dedicated ourselves to crafting cinematic experiences that resonate deeply with audiences. From concept to final cut, our mission is to turn ideas into unforgettable stories that leave an indelible mark on the world of film and entertainment.

With years of expertise in every facet of the filmmaking process, we specialize in creating compelling narratives, breathtaking visuals, and immersive soundscapes that transport viewers to new realms of imagination. Whether it's a feature film, documentary, commercial, or digital series, we approach each project with creativity, precision, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our experience in machine learning, AI, and immersive technologies allows us to reimagine storytelling for a new era. By exploring AR and XR through platforms like Microsoft HoloLens and Google Glass, we’ve crafted experiences that blur the line between reality and imagination. These tools let us transport audiences beyond the screen, creating narratives that are not only watched, but truly lived.

Join us on a journey where creativity knows no bounds, and the art of storytelling takes center stage. Welcome to Manikyam Productions, where innovation meets emotion, and every frame tells a tale.

Vishnu Satya  

Vishnu is a writer, director, and producer with an MFA in Directing from the University of Southern California’s School of Cinematic Arts. His passion for filmmaking, rooted in a childhood love of theater and storytelling, has grown into a clear vision for creating compelling feature films.

A seasoned communicator and former Toastmasters International speaker, Vishnu brings both creativity and discipline to his work. He has also served as a reader and judge for festivals such as Cinequest and the Austin Film Festival, providing professional script coverage. With a focus on excellence and efficiency in every stage of production, Vishnu is dedicated to delivering films that resonate deeply with audiences worldwide.
